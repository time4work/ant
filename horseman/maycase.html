Sep 21, 11:20 am
View Thumbnails for Images

If your firm handles lots of photos and images, you’ll be happy to know that we’ve added thumbnails for images.  Now when you’re trying to scan the hundreds of photos that your client just uploaded for you, you can more easily find what you’re looking for!  This will work for .jpg and .png files on both the Documents tab and the Case Details Documents section.  

Sep 15, 9:16 am
Apply Interest to Unpaid Balances

Now you apply interest through batch billing and individual invoice creation. When creating invoices, the adjustments section offers the ability to add an interest adjustment based on balances forwarded (unpaid invoice balances). This feature is available to all users who have access to creating invoices.

Sep 07, 10:55 am
Notes will now AutoSave

Never lose an in-progress Note again!  The new Notes AutoSave feature will automatically save the notes you’re working on in real time; just remember to select to which Client/Contact/Case/Matter the Note is linked.  Please note that autosaved draft notes are only visible to you until you post that Note.

Aug 30, 8:09 am
Now View Your Fields Available for Templates Directly in the App

We’ve made it easier to set up a new Template by making it easy to see which fields you can utilize.  On the Templates tab, under the gear icon, select “View Merge Fields” and get a full listing of all your available data fields in MyCase.  From there you can copy those fields and paste them directly into your template.  This replaces the previous “Download Merge Fields” .txt file process.  

Aug 17, 1:22 pm
Snooze and Individually Dismiss Reminders

We want to make sure you don’t miss important events or tasks, so we’ve added the ability to snooze and dismiss calendar and event reminders.  Not ready to take care of a task?  Snooze it for a few minutes, hours or even days.  Completed a calendar event but still want to remember another upcoming event?  Dismiss one and snooze the other.
